The WCTF Framework

Worth Climbing Together Framework (WCTF) treats job evaluation like planning a mountain expedition. Every job opportunity is a mountain to climb, and success requires more than just a viable business - you need the right peak, the right team, sustainable conditions, and a story worth telling.

The Mountain Metaphor - Five Dimensions:

  1. Mountain Range - Can any team succeed here? (Business viability, market position, financial health)
  2. Chosen Peak - Is everyone climbing the same mountain? (Technical strategy, engineering culture, alignment, coordination style matches terrain demands ‚Üê NEW v3.1)
  3. Rope Team Confidence - Does the team believe they can succeed together? (Leadership trust, team quality, organizational stability)
  4. Daily Climb - Will the 99 days be energizing or draining? (Work-life balance, compensation, daily sustainability)
  5. Story Worth Telling - Would you be proud to explain this work? (Career narrative, impact, skills growth)

Two-Layer Investigation:

  • Layer 1 (Public Research): Automated research on financials, market position, organizational news, technical culture - creates baseline understanding and surfaces obvious red flags.
  • Layer 2 (Insider Conversations): Conversations with current/former employees to validate Layer 1 findings, understand ground truth vs PR, and surface cultural nuances.

Flag-Based Philosophy: WCTF does not produce numerical scores or automated recommendations. Instead, it surfaces green flags (positive signals) and red flags (concerns) to inform your gut decision. Context matters - the same fact can be different colors depending on your priorities. There are no perfect companies, only trade-offs.
