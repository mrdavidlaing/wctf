## Company Overview

Ditto is an edge-native mobile database company that's redefining how data moves at the edge. With over $145 million in funding, they enable resilient device connectivity and data synchronization across mobile and edge environments without relying solely on cloud infrastructure.

### Mission
To help businesses "build flexible operations that are both latency-sensitive and resilient without reliance on network hardware, edge servers, or the cloud"

### Key Technology
- **Offline-first mobile database** with CRDT-powered conflict resolution
- **Peer-to-peer mesh networking** - devices automatically discover and connect
- **Multi-protocol sync** across BLE, WiFi, LAN
- **Edge-native architecture** - works seamlessly in disconnected environments

### Notable Customers

- Japan Airlines
